# Privacy Policy

**Local Music Video Player**

**Last Updated: January 28, 2026**

## Introduction

Welcome to Local Music Video Player ("we", "our", or "the App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide

- **Media Files**: Music, video files, and related metadata (title, artist, album, artwork) that you import into the App
- **Playlists**: Playlist names and configurations you create
- **Lyrics**: Lyrics files you manually import
- **Settings**: Your app preferences and configurations

### Information Collected Automatically

- **Playback History**: Recently played tracks (stored locally only)
- **Network Information**: Local IP address (only used for WiFi Transfer feature, not transmitted externally)

### Information We Do NOT Collect

- Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
- Location data

## How We Use Your Information

All information is used solely to provide the App's functionality:

- **Media Playback**: To play your music and video files
- **Library Organization**: To display and organize your media library
- **Playlist Management**: To save and manage your playlists
- **Lyrics Display**: To show lyrics during playback
- **WiFi Transfer**: To enable file transfers between your devices on the same local network

## Data Storage

### Local Storage Only

All your data is stored **exclusively on your device**:

- Media files are stored in the App's Documents folder
- Playlists and preferences are stored in local databases
- Artwork and thumbnails are cached locally
- No data is uploaded to our servers

### Cloud Services (Optional)

If you choose to connect cloud services:

- **WebDAV/SMB**: Credentials are stored locally on your device
- **DLNA**: Only discovers devices on your local network
- We do not have access to your cloud service credentials or files

## Third-Party Services

### Lyrics Search (Optional)

When you search for lyrics online, the App may connect to:

- **LrcLib** (lrclib.net): An open lyrics database
- **QQ Music API**: For Chinese song lyrics

These services receive only the song title and artist name for search purposes. No personal information is transmitted.

## Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures:

- All data remains on your device
- Network transfers (WiFi Transfer) occur only on your local network
- Cloud service credentials are stored securely using iOS Keychain (where applicable)

## Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

- **Access**: All your data is visible within the App
- **Delete**: You can delete any media, playlist, or data at any time
- **Export**: Your files remain in standard formats and can be exported
- **Uninstall**: Removing the App deletes all associated data

## Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The App is a media player with no social features or data collection mechanisms.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
